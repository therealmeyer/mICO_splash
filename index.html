Background Info

Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) are an evolving industry. As with any growing enterprise, third parties intervene to create specialized services catered towards clients. Here at mICO, we firmly believe that creating a central mobile platform for viewing ICOS will allow coin issuers and investors to focus more on the product and less on the details of the actual sale.
